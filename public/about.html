About the studio

Built for riders
who want more
from their bike.

Apex Skin Studio is a boutique sportbike wrap shop serving NYC, Jersey City, and North Jersey — focused on one thing: giving your machine a finish that looks intentional, lasts, and can be changed again later.

What we solve

Repaint isn't
the only answer.

Most riders hit the same wall: the bike runs fine, but the plastics tell a different story.

Faded fairings, scuffed tanks, and factory colors that never felt like yours. A full repaint means high cost, long downtime, and a permanent decision. Cheap wraps peel at the edges within a season because the prep was skipped.

Vinyl wrap sits in the middle — custom color, surface protection, and the option to change your mind later. The challenge is finding a shop that understands sportbike geometry, not just flat automotive panels.

The process
How we're different

Documented.
Repeatable.
Transparent.

Every build follows the same five-step process — teardown, prep, wrap, reassemble, reveal.

That process is filmed and published so you can see the standard of work before you commit. No hidden prep. No mystery pricing. No treating your fairings like a car door.

Boutique capacity means fewer bikes in the queue and more attention on edge work, post-heating, and finish quality — the details that determine whether a wrap lasts one season or five.

What we specialize in

Sportbikes.
Nothing else.

Fairing curves, tank lines, tail cowls — the shapes that separate a clean wrap from a lifted mess.

Apex Skin Studio works exclusively on sportbikes: full wraps, fairing sets, tank and tail accents, helmets, and small parts. The F4i build series on the channel exists as a reference build — proof of the process applied to a real machine with real wear.

Whether you want a full color change, a gloss black accent, or a race livery, the goal is the same: a finish that looks factory-intentional up close and holds up to sun, heat, and daily riding.
