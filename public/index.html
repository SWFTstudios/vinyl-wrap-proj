The problem

Your bike still runs.
It just doesn't look
like you anymore.

Sun-faded plastics, scuffed tank paint, and OEM colors that never matched your style — most riders live with it because a repaint feels too expensive, too permanent, or too risky.

Vinyl wrap solves that. Change the color, protect the surface underneath, and reverse it later if you want something different. No spray booth. No weeks off the road. Just a clean, custom finish applied panel by panel.

The catch: motorcycle fairings are complex curves, tight edges, and high heat. A bad wrap lifts in a season. Apex Skin Studio exists to do it right — sportbike-specific, prep-heavy, and documented so you know exactly what you're getting.
