The fine print, plainly

What's included.
What's not.

Included

  • Panel removal and reinstallation
  • Full surface clean, prep, and decontamination
  • Professional application with wrapped edges
  • Post-heat and edge-seal for durability
  • Final inspection and care instructions

Not included

  • Paint or bodywork repair on damaged panels
  • Mechanical work, fasteners, or replacement parts
  • Guaranteed results on cracked or peeling surfaces
  • Removal of a previous low-quality wrap (quoted separately)
  • Shipping — this is local, in-person work for now
