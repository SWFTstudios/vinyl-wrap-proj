DAY 42

Metallic flake reads different in daylight

Reveal shots at dawn showed how garage lighting hides color shift. Documented for future material selection — so customers know what to expect before choosing a finish.

DAY 35

Tail cowl compound curves need patience

Three application passes on the tail section before the edges held clean. Post-heating and rework are part of the process — and part of what separates a lasting wrap from a lifted one.

DAY 21

Prep took longer than application

Twenty years of road film and chain lube on the mounts. Surface prep is the majority of labor on any wrap — and the reason edges hold or fail. No shortcuts here.
